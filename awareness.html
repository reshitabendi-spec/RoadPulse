🚦 Road Safety Awareness

Learn • Follow • Prevent

🪖 Helmet Safety

Wearing a helmet significantly reduces the risk of head injuries during accidents. Every rider and pillion rider should wear a certified helmet.

🚗 Seat Belt Safety

Seat belts protect passengers from serious injuries. Always wear a seat belt, even on short trips.

⚡ Over Speeding

Over speeding increases accident risk . Follow speed limits and drive responsibly. It may be fun to do that but it costs life.

📱No Mobile Usage while Driving

Using a mobile phone while driving distracts the driver.This can increase the risk of accidents and endanger both the driver and others.

🏍️ Rash Driving

Rash driving puts everyone at risk. Donot entertain rash driving. It can cause severe accidents and endanger lives.

🚦 Traffic Signal Discipline

Obey all traffic lights and road signs. Do not jump red signals. Respect pedestrian crossings

🍺 Drunk and Drive

Always remember donot drink and drive because alcohol affects vehicle contol and reduces concentration. Choose a safe ride home instead of driving after drinking

🌧️ Driving in Bad Weather

Slow down during rain or fog. Keep headlights on when visibility is low. Increase distance between vehicles.
